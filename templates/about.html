Our Story

Welcome to Coffee Shop , where passion for exceptional coffee meets a warm, inviting atmosphere. Our journey began in 1990 with a simple dream: to create a space where coffee lovers could savor the perfect cup of coffee while feeling right at home.

Founded by Behar Mahmuti, our coffee shop quickly became a cornerstone of the community. We take pride in sourcing the finest coffee beans from sustainable farms and perfecting every cup with expert craftsmanship.

Whether you're here to work, catch up with friends, or enjoy a quiet moment to yourself, Coffee Shop is more than just a café—it's your home away from home.

Awards and Recognition

At Coffee Shop, our passion for coffee and dedication to excellence have earned us several prestigious accolades over the years. These awards reflect our commitment to quality, innovation, and creating memorable experiences for our customers

- Best Coffee Shop of the Year 2023
Presented by the Global Coffee Excellence Awards, this honor celebrates our consistent quality, innovative brewing techniques, and outstanding customer service.
- Customer Choice Award 2022
Voted by coffee lovers across the region, this award from Local Café Review Network highlights the trust and love our patrons have for our beverages and atmosphere.
- Barista Excellence Award 2020
Awarded by the National Barista Guild, this accolade acknowledges the skill, creativity, and passion of our talented team of baristas.
