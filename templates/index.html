Coffees

Macchiato

A macchiato is a classic Italian coffee drink that is known for its bold flavor and simplicity. The term "macchiato" means "stained" or "spotted" in Italian, which refers to the process of marking the espresso with a small amount of steamed milk.

Esspresso

Espresso is known for its strong and bold flavor. A typical shot of espresso is about 1 ounce (30 ml) and contains a higher concentration of coffee oils, sugars, and dissolved solids than regular brewed coffee.

Latte

A latte is a popular coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk. It's known for its smooth and creamy texture, often topped with a small layer of foam. The ratio of milk to espresso gives it a mild, rich flavor, making it less intense than a straight espresso but more robust than a cappuccino.
